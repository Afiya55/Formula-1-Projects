#Ferrari-Strategy-Game

🏎️ Ferrari Strategy Decision Game

This is a beginner Python Formula 1 strategy simulator inspired by real Ferrari pit wall decisions.

This interactive terminal game generates dynamic race scenarios using:

🌧️ Weather conditions

🚨 Safety Cars

🛞 Tyre compounds & degradation

🏁 Remaining laps

📻 Team radio messages

🌍 Different F1 tracks

Your job is to make the perfect strategy call:

👉 Pit

OR

👉 Stay Out

The game evaluates your decisions based on realistic race conditions while tracking your score, streak, and round progression.

Built using Python, randomization, conditional logic, and F1 strategy concepts.

A Python-based Formula 1 strategy simulator inspired by real race engineering scenarios.
